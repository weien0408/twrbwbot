### What is Ranked Bedwars (RBW)? 

**Ranked Bedwars** is a competitive version of the popular Minecraft mini-game, Bedwars. It is primarily played on private servers and organized through Discord communities. Unlike casual games, RBW focuses on **high-level strategy, mechanical skill, and team coordination.**


### Key Features of RBW

* **ELO Rating System:** Players earn or lose points (ELO) based on whether they win or lose. This ensures you are matched with players of similar skill levels.
* **The "Sweaty" Meta:** The gameplay is much faster and more intense. Techniques like **speedbridging, block-hitting, and advanced movement** are mandatory requirements.
* **Strategic Roles:** Teams are usually split into specific roles, such as the **"Icarus"** (rusher), the **"Defender"** (bed protector), and the **"Support."**
* **Strict Rules:** To keep the game fair, RBW often bans certain "annoying" items found in casual games, like Fireballs, Silverfish, or Obsidian.

### Why People Play RBW

While casual Bedwars can be chaotic and luck-based, RBW is all about **skill expression.** It provides a platform for the best players in the world to prove their dominance and improve their mechanics in a disciplined environment.

### Common RBW Terminology 

| Term  | Definition  |
| --- | --- |
| **ELO** | The numerical value representing your rank/skill level. |
| **Queue (Que)** | Joining a matchmaking pool to find a game. |
| **Bridge Fight** | A high-stakes battle that happens on narrow bridges.  |
| **Base Trade** | When both teams lose their beds at the same time. |
